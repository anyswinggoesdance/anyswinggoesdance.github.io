Any Swing Goes Dance's goal is to promote social dance in the Indianapolis area that is both fun and affordable. Our primary dance is West Coast Swing. However we also include ballroom and country. We offer two dances a month. Both offer the choice of either beginner or intermediate dance instruction by accomplished instructors. During the evening you receive a group lesson, three hours of social dancing and food & soft drinks. A number of times each year we feature both nationally and internationally known dance professional's as guest instructors.

Charlotte Heiney, Owner
